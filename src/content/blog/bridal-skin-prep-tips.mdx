# Radiant Skin: The Foundation of Every Bridal Look

The most stunning makeup starts months before the wedding day. As a professional artist, I can tell you that **skin prep** is 70% of the battle.

## The 3-Month Countdown

### 1. Hydration is Key
Drink at least 3 liters of water daily. Your skin's internal hydration shows on the surface.

### 2. Professional Consultation
Schedule a visit with a dermatologist or a trusted aesthetician. Treat any concerns like acne or pigmentation early.

### 3. Consistency Over Intensity
Don't try new, harsh chemicals a week before the wedding. Stick to a gentle CTM (Cleanse, Tone, Moisturize) routine that your skin loves.

## The Night Before
- **Sleep**: 8 hours of restful sleep is non-negotiable.
- **Hydrating Mask**: Use a gentle sheet mask to lock in moisture.
- **Lip Care**: Exfoliate and balm your lips for a smooth lipstick application.

Remember, my goal is to enhance your natural beauty. The better you care for your skin, the more magic we can create together!
