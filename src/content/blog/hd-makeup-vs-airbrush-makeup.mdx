# HD vs. Airbrush: Decoding the Technical Side of Beauty

One of the most frequent questions I get from my brides is: *"Should I go for HD makeup or Airbrush?"* Both have their unique benefits, and the choice depends on your skin type, the venue, and your personal preference.

## What is HD Makeup?
HD (High-Definition) makeup is applied manually using high-end products specifically formulated to look invisible to high-definition cameras. It provides a natural, skin-like finish while offering full coverage.

## What is Airbrush Makeup?
Airbrushing involves spraying a fine mist of foundation onto the face using a specialized air compressor tool. It creates a very thin, even layer that is incredibly durable and sweat-proof.

### Comparison Table

| Feature | HD Makeup | Airbrush Makeup |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Application** | Manual (Brush/Sponge) | Air Compressor Mist |
| **Finish** | Natural & Radiant | Very Smooth & Matte |
| **Durability** | High | Extremely High |
| **Skin Type** | All types | Best for Oily/Combo |

In the Kerala climate, both are great options, but we often recommend Airbrush for long outdoor ceremonies due to its superior moisture resistance.
